Notes to grader: This world is a flower garden world, with a courtyard in the center and a maze of hedges around the edge. I modified the 2D hard-coded array so it includes "special" numbers that will generate specific features other than wall blocks. For example, a 5 on the map generates a stepping stone and an 8 on the map generates a tree. I also added a game feature to the world so users can press the "Maze" button, which will take them to a random corner of the map and they must find their way back to the center (or cheat by pressing the "Reset" button). I also added add/delete block buttons that will add/delete a block if possible. This function calculates the angle of the camera to determine which quadrant the user is currently looking at to determine where to add/delete the block. I also added randomized flowers to the center of the map. Each time the world is reloaded, a flower map is generated wit hrandom numbers and, depending on the threshhold set by the slider, will populate the spaces with flowers. For the "wow" factor, I tried to make my world very pretty and detailed. I also drew all of my own textures, so every texture in my world from the hedges to the cloudy sky was drawn by me in a pixel drawing editor.

Please use a browser that supports "canvas"
