Notes to grader: This rainbow trout is made of 68 transformed cubes. Its basic animation is tail, mouth, and front fin movement to create a swimming motion. On a shift-click, the fish will do a special animation where it swims faster, gets an angry expression, and eats a small fish that appears from off screen. The tail has 5 joints that are controllable by the user.

Please use a browser that supports "canvas"
