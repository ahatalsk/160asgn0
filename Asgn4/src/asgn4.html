Notes to grader: For this assignment I added both a point light and a spotlight to my blocky world. The point light takes in a position and color, and calculates the ambient, diffuse, and specular lighting. It can be toggled on/off, animated, and the position/color are adjustable. The spotlight takes in a position, a target, and a radius, and increases the brightness of any fragment within a certain angle of the target-position vector. It can be toggled on/off, animated, and the radius is adjustable. It is especially visible in top view. The blocky world still maintains full functionality.

Please use a browser that supports "canvas"
